Our Founders

Daksh Manchanda

Daksh Manchanda was born in Delhi, a city of dreams, but his mind always raced beyond reality. A tech enthusiast with an unrelenting passion for innovation, he was the kind of person who saw possibilities where others saw limits. His creativity knew no bounds—wild daydreams often turned into bold experiments. An extrovert by nature, he thrived in conversations, always learning, always evolving. Driven by an ambition to be the Tony Stark of his generation, Daksh turned his futuristic ideas into reality, building a company that reflected his vision—where technology met imagination, and the impossible became possible.

Parv Rastogi

Parv Rastogi, born in Delhi, is a visionary entrepreneur with an insatiable passion for innovation. A big tech geek and an avid Shark Tank fan, he thrives on learning and pushing boundaries. From a young age, he aspired to break the conventional mold, determined to carve his own path. His entrepreneurial journey began with a relentless drive to create something impactful, fueled by his love for technology and business. As a co-founder, he embodies resilience and creativity, always seeking the next big breakthrough. For Parv, success isn't just about building a company—it's about transforming ideas into reality.

Our Story

2015

Parv and Daksh cross paths as college roommates—two restless minds refusing to follow the herd. While their batchmates chase placements, they dream of building something bigger. Conversations at midnight turn into business plans by dawn. Brique & Bois is born, an audacious leap into the construction industry.

2017

With boundless passion but limited funds, they pitch their vision to investors—only to be met with rejection after rejection. Their savings drain, their company teeters on the edge. Doubt creeps in, but quitting isn't in their DNA.

2019

Just when collapse seems inevitable, a venture capitalist sees the spark others missed. With fresh funding and guidance, Brique & Bois gets a second chance. The duo refines their strategy, turning their startup into a force to be reckoned with.

2021

Projects start rolling in, and so does recognition. Their bold, tech-driven approach disrupts traditional construction norms. They're no longer just "those college kids with a startup"—they're industry challengers.

2023

Scaling up means thinking bigger. They launch an app that streamlines construction workflows, setting a new industry standard. Innovation becomes their signature, and competitors start taking notes.

2024

What started as a dorm room dream is now a powerhouse. With a workforce of 1 lakh employees, Brique & Bois shapes skylines and redefines modern construction. The journey was anything but easy, but for Parv and Daksh, this is just the beginning.
